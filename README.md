# Maa-I (माई): Voice-First AI Maternal Health Companion

## 📌 Project Overview
Maa-I is an AI-driven health companion designed for pregnant women, new mothers, and families in underserved regions of India. The project addresses the "last-mile" delivery of healthcare information by converting complex medical protocols into actionable, culturally nuanced voice guidance in regional languages.

## ⚠️ The Problem: The Information-Action Gap
In many underserved communities, reliable health information fails to reach the end user due to:
* **Literacy Barriers:** Medical instructions are often text-heavy and inaccessible to those with low literacy.
* **Language Gaps:** Standard health apps rarely support the hyper-local dialects used in rural India.
* **Overburdened Infrastructure:** ASHA workers handle high caseloads (avg. 30 visits/day), leading to fatigue and potential counseling errors.
* **Delayed Care-Seeking:** Preventable "danger signs" are often ignored or attributed to myths, leading to late-stage medical interventions.

## 🚀 The Solution: A "Continuous Care" Model
Maa-I moves beyond static information retrieval to provide a proactive health ecosystem:

### 1. AI-Enabled Voice Assistance (Active Querying)
A natural language interface that allows users to speak their symptoms or concerns and receive immediate, reliable medical guidance.

### 2. Proactive Health Nudges
Instead of waiting for the user to engage, Maa-I sends weekly personalized voice "nudges" regarding nutrition, medication adherence, and upcoming ANC (Antenatal Care) visits.

### 3. Smart Symptom Triage
An AI-powered triage engine that detects high-risk symptoms and triggers immediate alerts for the user, their family, and local health workers.

### 4. Postpartum & Infant Care
Extends support for two years following birth, tracking infant growth milestones and providing maternal mental health guidance.

## 👥 User Personas & Impact
* **The Rural Mother:** Receives life-saving guidance in her own language without needing to read or write.
* **The ASHA Worker:** Uses Maa-I as a digital aid to provide accurate counseling and reduce referral errors.
* **The Husband/Family:** Receives automated alerts when medical attention is critically needed, facilitating faster household decision-making.

## 🛠️ Product Strategy (MoSCoW)
* **Must Have:** Voice guidance in local languages, AI smart triage, offline access for essential content, and ANC/Vaccination reminders.
* **Should Have:** SOS emergency button and teleconsultation integration.
* **Could Have:** Community support groups and personalized nutrition planners based on local food availability.

## 📊 Success Metrics (North Star)
* **North Star Metric:** % of users who receive proactive, actionable guidance before a clinically defined complication window.
* **Engagement:** Weekly active listeners and adherence rate to ANC/Immunization schedules.
* **Impact:** Reduction in "danger sign" escalation time and ASHA worker productivity improvement.
